Pig

Pigs are domesticated animals known for their intelligence and social behavior. They are often raised for their meat, but they also play important roles in agriculture and as pets.

Pigs are omnivores, meaning they eat both plants and animals. They have a keen sense of smell and are known for their ability to root in the ground for food. Pigs are also highly social animals that live in groups called sounders.

In many cultures, pigs are symbols of prosperity and good fortune. They are also featured in various folklore and stories around the world.

Fun Fact: Pigs are considered one of the smartest domesticated animals, capable of learning tricks and commands similar to dogs.
